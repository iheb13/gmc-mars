On the exciting planet of Mars, there are plenty of different activities you can do by yourself or with your family that will keep you busy all day long. You can start off your day with an excellent in-depth scavenger hunt through the Martian channels. After that, at around lunchtime, you and the family can have a delicious picnic while watching Mars’ beautiful seasonal color variations. Then, for some real excitement, you can bungee jump into Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the Galaxy. Next, you can bring the whole family to one of Mars’ impact craters where you can enjoy some fabulous cuisine at one of many delicious "crater café’s". Finally, you can end your eventful day with a fun and relaxing ice skating session on Mars’ polar caps.
